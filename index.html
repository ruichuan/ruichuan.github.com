Ruichuan Chen

Researcher @ Bell Labs

I am a researcher at Bell Labs in Stuttgart, Germany. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher working with Paul Francis at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Peking University in 2009.

My research interests span the broad areas of networking and distributed systems. In particular, my current research centers around cloud computing and data analytics.

Openings. We have some openings for research interns in the areas of cloud computing and data analytics. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
