Ruichuan Chen

Researcher @ Bell Labs

I am a researcher at Bell Labs in Stuttgart, Germany. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher working with Paul Francis at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Peking University in 2009.

My research interests span the broad areas of networking and distributed systems. In particular, I am currently interested in using systems approaches to address security, privacy and reliability issues in various networked and distributed systems.

Internship. At Bell Labs Stuttgart, we are actively looking for highly motivated research interns. Feel free to send me an email if you are interested.
