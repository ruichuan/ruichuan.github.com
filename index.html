I am a Distinguished Member of Technical Staff (DMTS) at Bell Labs. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher working with Paul Francis at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Peking University.

My research interests span the broad areas of networking and distributed systems. In particular, my current research centers around cloud computing, data analytics, and machine learning systems. I open source whenever possible.

Openings. We have openings for research interns in the areas of cloud computing and decentralized systems all year round. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
